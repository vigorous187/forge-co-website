## The Starting Point

MTC Renovations had strong service execution and real customer results, but the web layer was carrying operational debt:

- indexation handling was inconsistent across routes and content types
- sitemap management depended on manual updates
- SEO requirements were not consistently enforced during build

That made growth fragile. Small content updates could silently create crawl issues.

## What We Changed

Forge implemented a system-first baseline:

- automated sitemap generation tied to actual content sources
- build-time SEO checks for canonical coverage, robots behavior, and noindex safety
- repeatable guardrails so regressions fail fast instead of leaking into production

## Outcome

MTC moved from ad hoc SEO fixes to a maintainable operating model where technical quality is enforced by default.

That gives the brand a reliable base for compounding content and local search growth.
